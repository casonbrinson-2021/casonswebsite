Movies iOS App

A simple yet useful app that manages your movies for you. This app organizes, saves, displays, and searches for the movies you want to know about. All of this is possible through the connection to two API's full of movie information. The screenshots down below detail what each screen of the app accomplishes.

Home Tab

A simple home tab display an image slider and give credit to the API's used.

Favorites Tab

An informative view of movies that the user has favorited. Also able to be searched.

Clicking into any of the favorited movies brings you to that selection's info screen.

There are various links to view more information such as the movie trailer and cast member list.

Continued display of information about this favorited movie.

Now Playing Tab

A list view of now playing movies pulled from the API.

Clicking on any of these movies brings you to that selection's info screen.

This info screen is similair to the one seen on the Favorites Tab. However, this view allows you to add your seleciton to your favorites list.

Continued display of information about the movie.

Search Tab

A search screen to search through the database of movies available.

The user can view search results by clicking the search button.

Clicking on a result shows you that movies information screen.

Genres Tab

A view of all of the user's favorited movies that is able to be filtered and sorted by genre.
